Recording checklist

  1. Tool: Loom, QuickTime, or OBS. Loom gives an instant shareable link.
  2. Rehearse twice. The lines are timed for about 150 words per minute.
  3. 1920x1080, show the cursor, slow your clicks so viewers can follow.
  4. Let animations finish (the hub network, the multi-modal charts) before talking over them.
  5. Add captions. Many judges watch muted.
  6. Keep the disclaimer line. Put the hub URL in the submission description.
